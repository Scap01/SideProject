Motorcycle driving instructor

​​You have a passion for teaching and you're an experienced driver? Become a driving instructor at Tecnic and be part of the largest driving school in Québec.

By becoming a motorcycle driving instructor at Tecnic:

  • Share your passion for motorcycles with people that are eager to learn;
  • Learn and exchange with colleagues that are as passionate as you are;
  • Stay away from routine work;
  • Take advantage of a flexible schedule.

We are looking for someone that is:

  • Rigorous and mindful of their work;
  • Patient, qualified, that loves working with people;
  • Driven by the thought of improving Québec's road safety record;
  • Motivated by seeing their students succeed!

Training and requirements

  • High School Diploma or any relevant work experience.
  • Hold a class 6A driver’s licence valid in Quebec for at least 2 years and have less than 4 demerit points
