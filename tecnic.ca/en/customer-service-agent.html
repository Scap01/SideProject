Customer service agent

You will see! It’s really gratifying to help our youth by making a difference in this important time of their lives.

By becoming a customer service agent at Tecnic:

  • Play an important role in our business by advising our customers.
  • Work in a warm atmosphere.
  • Take advantage of a paid training with essential working tools.
  • Treat yourself with a stable job close to your home.
  • Take advantage of a good quality of life with a flexible schedule for full-time and part-time positions.

We are looking for someone that is:

  • Rigorous and mindful of their work.
  • Patient, cheerful, that loves working with people.
  • Mature, responsible and autonomous.
  • Able to work with Microsoft Office suite.

Training and requirements

  • Have a High School Diploma or DEP in sales
  • Hold a driver’s licence
  • Sales technique skills an asset
  • Bilingual an asset
