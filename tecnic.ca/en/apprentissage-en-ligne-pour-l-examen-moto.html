Motocycle exam online learning

Prepare for the SAAQ’s motorcycle knowledge test from the comfort of your own home!

Tecnic, Québec’s top driving school since 1985, has developed over time the experience and expertise that will help you pass your motorcycle driving course with flying colours and learn how to be a safe, courteous and responsible driver.
​
​​​

Online learning: an exclusive product for Tecnic driving school clients

This learning tool is one of many pedagogical methods developed to help Tecnic’s motorcycle clients successfully pass the SAAQ’s knowledge test. Tecnic clients also have access to other learning resources, including a motorcycle driving booklet, a series of preparatory questions for the SAAQ knowledge test (5 questionnaires with 32 questions each) and classroom courses. These resources were developed to help our motorcycle students learn everything they need to know in order to successfully pass the SAAQ exam required to obtain a motorcycle driver’s licence.

For more information regarding our tools and resources, contact the Tecnic motorcycle school in your region.
